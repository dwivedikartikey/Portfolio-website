Life as a Tier 3 college student in India.

This story is going to sound similiar to most of student who study in a tier 3 college as every student is not as brilliant as to get a tier 1 college and if he is tier 1 college don't have that much of seats in college.

Some students work hard in there eleventh and twelfth standard to get a good tier 1 college while others accept the fact that tier 3 college is there destiny.
Though this journey is about me and how i spent these four years of my life.

Many students think that college life is fun and you get full freedom to do anything you want but bursting your bubble it's not that easy. Atleast tier 3 student life is not that easy for one who wants to do something good in his/her life. It's just that during the initial phase of his life (i.e. 12th standard) he can't figure it out and that's totally fine.

My journey was also same as I don't have any idea of what to do after 12th standard.

But as this remain similar for most of tier 3 student that in college we waste most of our time in fun. Same goes with me and when I realised I started to work on myself by learning new technology and being best what I can do. Though my journey is near to end in my college and Let's figure out what most I can make out of it.

One last and final advise to every junior who is entering into the college life is "Start Early, Start Now". As time is running fast.
